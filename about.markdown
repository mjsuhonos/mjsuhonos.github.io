I am an information scientist and librarian working at [Toronto Metropolitan University](https://library.torontomu.ca).

Fifteen years ago, I worked as a "metadata librarian" and had a short-lived blog about it. It was a place where I wrote ideas related to my work as a way to share them with other people who might find them interesting. This site is another attempt at that.
